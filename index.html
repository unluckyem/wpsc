WHITNEY PIER SOCCER CLUB

Youth soccer club based in Whitney Pier, NS. We offer summer soccer programs for children aged U4 - U17. Our programs follow the Canada Soccer pathways and encourage sportsmanship, physical literacy, and teamwork. Whitney Pier Soccer is recognized as a Quality Soccer Provider in Canada Soccer's National Youth Club Licensing Program.
